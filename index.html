What is Cognitive Debt?

Cognitive debt is the accumulation of unresolved mental tasks, stressors, and negative thought patterns that reduce mental performance.

Causes

  • Multitasking
  • Procrastination
  • Stress
  • Overthinking

Effects

  • Poor concentration
  • Anxiety
  • Burnout
